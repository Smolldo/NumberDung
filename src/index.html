Game Of Numbers

Rules:

  1. Select a name.
  2. By pressing the 'generate' button, you and the AI ​​will have random numbers.
  3. Whoever has the highest number wins the round.
  4. The game lasts up to 3 wins.
  5. In case of a tie, an additional round is held.
  6. Good luck, Samurai.
